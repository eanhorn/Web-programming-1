My Education

Stevens

My favorite course at Stevens (Aside from Web Programming) was Principles of Programming Languages. Many people struggled with the course, but Dr. Bonelli taught it in a way that helped me grasp a pretty difficult language, and by the end of the class we had built our own programming language

My most memorable experience at Stevens was when I first came here in my junior year and lived away from home. I had never lived away from home before, and it took some adjusting along with the courseload before I was comfortable. I will never forget what it was like starting my journey here at Stevens.

Ocean County College

My favorite course from OCC was my Data Structures course. I learned so much from my professor and he taught the class in a way that made perfect sense to me.

Having to attend this school online was very memorable for me, as I had never done any online classes before, and making such a quick transition was not easy and very stressful.
