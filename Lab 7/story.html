The time I drove forever...

The time was just after the pandemic was coming out of its worst. My friends and I had tried to plan trips during the start of the pandemic, but COVID restrictions had other plans for us. After multiple attempts at taking a long trip, the opportunity finally arose in the spring of 2021 when the peak of the pandemic had already passed. We planned our trip, and when the day come my friends and I packed our car tight and began the drive for Ohio. We were going to Sandusky to spend a weekend on Lake Erie.

We started the long journey, having 8 hours to get there and more sunlight behind us than ahead. I started the drive, powering through the first 3 hours without stopping. Long drives always excited me, but being in one was not so fun. The drive got boring quickly, and my energy was dwindling. My friend took over the drive for a few hours, and we traded off a few times before we got there. The drive felt like it would never end, but we began to cruise through the Pennsylvania and Ohio countryside, seeing marvelous sights along the way. With the sun long set and some of my friends out for the count, we arrived at our hotel and enjoyed our stay. While the drive was no easy task, it is one I truly enjoyed and would do again in a heartbeat if given the opportunity.
